========== DRIVE STICK ==========
		
		
  Left Stick: Drive left wheels
 Right Stick: Drive right wheels
		
           X: Reverse drive direction
           Y: Toggle Single Drivestick mode
Right Bumper: Portcullis Automation
 Left Bumper: Cheval De Frise Automation
       Start: Toggle Ramping code (Smoothes drive. May need to turn off for crossing the Moat)
       
       
       
========== CONTROL STICK ==========

 Right Stick: Move arm Up/Down (Up = Into the robot | Down = Towards the floor)

           A: Reset arm to highest position (Resets encoder)
          
 Left Bumper: Wheels in  (?) need to check
Right Bumper: Wheels out (?) need to check
